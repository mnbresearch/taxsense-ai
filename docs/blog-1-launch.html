What it does

You describe how you earn, the way you would tell a friend: "I make around 80k a month, pay 25k rent in Pune, put 1.5L in PPF, and sold some mutual funds for a 2 lakh gain." That single sentence is enough. TaxSense AI structures it, annualises it, and computes your full FY 2025-26 tax position under both the old and the new regime — slabs, HRA under Rule 2A, the Section 87A rebate with marginal relief, capital gains under 111A/112A with the ₹1.25 lakh exemption, surcharge, cess — with every section cited. No black box.

It doesn't just calculate. It advises.

  • ₹-quantified moves: not "invest in 80C" but "this exact move saves you ₹18,700" — every suggestion ranked by savings per rupee.
  • CTC Designer: the only tool we know of that designs the salary structure you should ask HR for — sweeping basic %, HRA and employer NPS to maximise take-home.
  • Business-owner ready: 44AD/44ADA presumptive guidance, audit triggers, GST registration pointers, an advance-tax calendar with 234B/234C interest, and a monthly Tax Jar that tells you how much to set aside.
  • 60-second Tax Guide: a tap-through wizard that tells freelancers and business owners exactly what they must file — before any numbers.
  • Filing-ready PDF: regime recommendation, quantified moves, installment calendar, document checklist. File it yourself or hand your CA a clean, complete file.

Private by design

Your numbers live behind row-level security — each user's data is isolated at the database level, and nothing you enter trains anyone's model. The tax math itself is a deterministic, section-cited rules engine covered by 97 automated tests; the AI only handles the conversation.
